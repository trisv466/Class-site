Clock

2018

Studio - Project


A clock can signify the passing of time through increasing its values, or decreasing them. I chose the latter as a theme for this project, where everyday objects (a bagel, a metrocard, and a cup of coffee) slowly disintegrate and disappear into nothingness as every one minute passes by. The idea that inspired me was the concept of increasing decay as time passes.

Responsive Page

2018

Studio - Workshop


For this workshop in creating responsive pages, I wanted my page to have more than just a stylistic change in every browser width. Therefore, I tried to make it interactive for the user: the stick figure appears as if it were “pushing” back the wall while the browser screen is reduced from the right.

Linking Tree

2018

Studio - Project


Given the prompt of nonlinear storytelling—starting from one page, leading to two, leading to four, and so on— I thought that this project was greatly appropriate to a “choose your adventure” sort of theme. Therefore, I chose to depict the storyline of the original 80s Super Mario Bros game in words instead of images, where the user can choose their own outcome of the story; playing it like a game.
