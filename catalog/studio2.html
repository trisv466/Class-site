Container Technologies

2018

Studio - Project


A human body may not be considered an enclosed location or space, but the numerous amounts of body parts that collectively work together make it a ‘container’ in its own way. I did not want to simply have a filter to separate these body parts based on category, so I decided to create a drag-and-drop game that users can play in order to determine the categories each part belongs to. The categories I chose were: internal / external, motor / static, nervous / nerve-less/ sensory-non-sensory.
