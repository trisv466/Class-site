Mix

2018

Lab - Project


For this project, we had to create a moving website as a music video for a song. I chose Mikky Ekko’s song Kids, and used gifs of the elderly dancing like children to convey the song title’s irony. I chose bright colors to go along with the fun spirit of the song.

All-html

2018

Lab - Project


As the class’s very first project, this was our chance to familiarize ourselves with all the available HTML tags. For my 5 pages that each had to incorporate an HTML tag, I chose the tags mark, unordered list, image, subscript, and table.

Filters

2018

Lab - Workshop


I chose the theme of global warming; using the current and upcoming years as filters. Humans and nature are the objects being ‘filtered’ each year to convey that one day, there will be no living creatures on earth as a result of global warming.

Self

2018

Lab - Workshop


The broad prompt of the concept of ‘self’ allowed me to explore the specific idea of figuring out the true identity of a self other than your own. The puzzles of various faces signify how truly complex ‘selves’ are, and that it may take some time for you to put the pieces of their ‘real self’ together.
