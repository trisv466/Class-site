Our body parts can be represented in more ways than one: through the literal, through symbols, through euphemisms, or through other objects they are often associated with. Additionally, the categories that they can be placed in are endless. This is a game that tests your knowledge on the categories your body parts fall in, and lets you experience the fun of guessing a body part based on its unconventional representation.
